Our Products

EP & NN Belt Range

Two proven carcass technologies tailored to different demands — choose the belt that matches your load, speed, and operating environment.

Polyester-Nylon · High Tensile
EP
Polyester Warp × Nylon Weft Fabric Carcass

EP belts deliver outstanding tensile strength with very low elongation, thanks to their polyester warp and nylon weft construction. Ideal for long-distance, high-tension conveyors where minimal belt stretch reduces drive energy and take-up requirements.

Tensile Strength
250 – 2000 N/mm
Ply Count
2 – 6 Plies
Belt Width
500 – 2000 mm
Cover Grades
M, N, S, HR, FR
Nylon-Nylon · High Flexibility
NN
Polyamide Warp × Polyamide Weft Fabric Carcass

NN belts use all-nylon (polyamide) construction in both warp and weft, giving them superior impact absorption and troughability. Their elasticity absorbs shock loading effectively — perfect for short, steep inclines and heavy impact loading zones.

Tensile Strength
250 – 1800 N/mm
Ply Count
2 – 6 Plies
Belt Width
500 – 2000 mm
Cover Grades
M, N, S, HR, FR
Cover Grade Reference
M
General Purpose

Standard abrasion resistance for moderate-duty conveying of non-abrasive to mildly abrasive materials.

N
Normal Duty

Medium abrasion resistance, suitable for general bulk material handling applications.

S
Super Abrasion Resistant

High abrasion resistance for sharp, coarse, or highly abrasive materials such as ore, clinker, and crushed stone.

HR
Heat Resistant

Designed for conveying hot materials such as clinker, coke, and sinter at elevated temperatures.

FR
Fire Resistant

Meets fire resistance requirements for underground mines and environments where fire safety is critical.

Where Our Belts Perform

From underground mines to port terminals — our belts are trusted in the most demanding material handling environments.

⛏️
Mining & Quarrying

Heavy-duty EP belts for underground coal, iron ore, limestone, and hard-rock applications with superior rip and gouge resistance.

🏗️
Cement & Construction

Heat-resistant and abrasion-grade belts for clinker, raw meal, and aggregate conveyors operating at elevated temperatures.

Power Plants

Fire-retardant EP belts for coal handling, ash conveyors, and fuel feed systems in thermal and captive power stations.

🌾
Fertiliser & Chemicals

Oil-resistant and chemical-resistant NN and EP belts for urea, DAP, potash, and bulk chemical conveying applications.

Long-length high-tension EP belts for ship loaders, stackers-reclaimers, and overland conveyors at bulk cargo terminals.

🍚
Food & Agro Processing

Food-grade NN belts with white covers for grain, sugar, salt, and commodity conveyors requiring hygienic material contact.
